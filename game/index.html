LD51 - Picoverse

How to play?
Use arrows to maneuver spacecraft around the planets. When you hit planet directly, you crash. However, when you land with feet first, you will land safely.
Play this on desktop only.

What is this game about?
Every 20 seconds, some random planet will be destroyed. You have to land on the planet before it gets destroyed and save people of that planet. The more people you save, the better you are in this game.
Also try not to destroy yourself.

What is Ludum Dare?
Ludum Dare is a compo, where you try to make a game in 48 hours, this time with theme "every 10 seconds".
